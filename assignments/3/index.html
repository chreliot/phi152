Assignment 3

In this assignment we use Whewell’s and Popper’s models of scientific reasoning and justification to understand scientific developments from the last couple months. For each of the following numbered items, write a short paragraph — at least several sentences.

  1. For one of the following Science News stories, describe (at least some important aspect of) the reasoning of the scientists who performed this research, in terms of William Whewell’s model of scientific method:
  • “An ancient impact on Earth led to a cascade of cratering”
  • “Homo sapiens may have reached Europe 10,000 years earlier than previously thought”
  1. For one of the following Science News stories, describe (at least some important aspect of) the reasoning of the scientists who performed this research, in terms of Karl Popper’s model of scientific method:
  • “Clovis hunters’ reputation as mammoth killers takes a hit”
  • “Organic molecules in an ancient Mars meteorite formed via geology, not alien life”
  • “Americans tend to assume imaginary faces are male”
  • “Here’s what goldfish driving ‘cars’ tell us about navigation”
  • “An arc of galaxies 3 billion light-years long may challenge cosmology”
  1. For one of the two analyses above — either your Whewell example or your Popper example, that is: In what ways could the example fit the model of scientific method better? Or, to put the same question a different way, In what ways might it not exactly fit the philosopher’s ideal?
