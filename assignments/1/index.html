Assignment 1

Upload your assignment to the Blackboard link for Assignments by Feb 21.

Tasks

  1. Produce your own original examples of:

    • a deductively valid, sound argument
    • a deductively valid argument which is not sound
    • an inductively strong argument
    • a version of that same argument which is even stronger
    • an argument which sounds a little plausible and might actually persuade someone not being careful about their reasoning, but which is neither deductively valid nor inductively strong

  2. Use your imagination and powers of observation a bit: What are some different ways inductive arguments can have more inductive strength than others? Discuss three ways; write at least 150 words.
