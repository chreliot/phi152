Online, open-access books to learn from

  • Odds & Ends: Introducing Probability & Decision with a Visual Emphasis, by Jonthan Weisberg
  • Data Visualization: A Practical Introduction, by Kieran Healy
  • Grinstead and Snell’s Introduction to Probability, by Peter G. Doyle, Charles M. Grinstead and J. Laurie Snell
  • OpenIntro Statistics, by David M. Diez et al.
  • Introductory Statistics for the Life and Biomedical Sciences, a derivative of OpenIntro, above
  • Online Statistics Education: An Interactive Multimedia Course of Study, by David M. Lane, et al.
  • SticiGui, by Philip B. Stark
  • Probability: Lectures and Labs, by Mark Huber

Stanford Encylopedia of Philosophy articles

The SEP is a high-quality, free resource.

  • Scientific Method, by Hanne Andersen and Brian Hepburn
  • Scientific Discovery, by Jutta Schickore
  • Scientific Reproducibility, by Fiona Fidler and John Wilcox
  • Scientific Research and Big Data, by Sabina Leonelli
  • Historicist Theories of Scientific Rationality, by Thomas Nickles
