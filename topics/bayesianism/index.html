Topic 4: Bayesianism

Bayes’s rule is a simple piece of math. It expreses a relationship between probabilities. But it has been deployed as the heart of a solution to inductive reasoning which is called “Bayesianism.” We will working on understanding, and then begin to assess, this solution.

Reading

  1. Hacking, Chapter 7 “Bayes’ Rule” (pages 69–77)
  2. Hacking, Chapter 13 “Personal Probabilities” (pages 151–160)
  3. Hacking, Chapter 14 “Coherence” (pages 163–169)

See also

  • Elliott Sober, “Introduction to Bayesian Epistemology”
