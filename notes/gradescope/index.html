Gradescope

For feedback and grades on your [Assignments] and [Tests] for this course, we will be using the web app Gradescope, which is newly supported by Hofstra.

Before you can view any of your graded submissions, you will need to create an account with Gradescope. You will receive an email directly from Gradescope with a link for logging in.

No student has yet reported having difficulty with this process, so—having not used it myself from the student—I think it’s pretty straightforward. But of course let me know if you have a problem. And if you need more instructions, here is an instructional video from Gradeschope called “For students: Viewing feedback & requesting regrades.“
